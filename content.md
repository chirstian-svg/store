-About HoloVault

HoloVault is a small New Zealand based Pokémon TCG singles store built by a collector, for collectors.

What started as a personal hobby quickly became a passion for finding great cards, helping others complete collections, and creating a store that feels honest, personal, and community focused.

Every card listed is carefully sourced, checked, photographed, and packed with care. We focus on clean condition notes, fair pricing, and making the buying experience simple and enjoyable — whether you’re chasing grails, favourite Pokémon, modern hits, or unique language variants.

At HoloVault, we know every order means something. Whether it’s your first single or the final card for your binder, we appreciate every collector who supports the store.

Thanks for being part of the journey 💜

-Returns & Refunds
HoloVault Returns Policy

At HoloVault, we are a small collector-focused Pokémon TCG singles store. Please choose carefully before purchasing, as we generally do not accept returns or change-of-mind purchases.

Due to the nature of trading cards, collectables, sealed products, and condition-sensitive items, all sales are considered final unless an item arrives significantly different from its listing description.

Condition & Listings

We do our best to provide:

Accurate card photos
Honest condition notes
Clear descriptions
Careful packaging

Please review all photos and descriptions carefully before placing an order.

Returns We May Accept

We may review return requests if:

The wrong item was sent
The item arrives damaged in transit
The item received is significantly different from the listing

If there is an issue with your order, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

Your order number
Photos of the item
Photos of packaging if damaged during shipping
Non-Returnable Items

We do not accept returns for:

Change of mind
Incorrect purchases
Minor condition differences
Grading expectations
Sealed product openings
Buyer remorse
Shipping Issues

Once orders are handed to the shipping provider, delivery times are outside of our control. However, we will always do our best to assist if an issue arises.

Contact

For any order concerns, please contact HoloVault through the store contact page or email provided on the website.



-Shipping Information
HoloVault Shipping Policy

HoloVault ships Pokémon TCG singles from New Zealand to collectors both locally and worldwide.

Every order is packed carefully using protective sleeves, top loaders, team bags, and secure packaging where appropriate to help ensure cards arrive safely.

Processing Times

Orders are typically packed and dispatched within 1–3 business days.

During busy periods, new releases, or weekends, dispatch times may be slightly longer.

New Zealand Shipping

Shipping rates are automatically calculated at checkout based on location and order size.

This may include:

Standard NZ shipping
Rural delivery
Signature-required shipping for higher-value orders

Tracking will be provided where available.

International Shipping

HoloVault ships worldwide to selected countries.

International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package size.

Please note:

Delivery times vary by country
Import duties, taxes, or customs fees are the buyer’s responsibility
Some countries may experience delays outside our control
Packaging

Cards are packed with care to help protect against damage during transit.

Depending on the order, packaging may include:

Penny sleeves
Top loaders
Semi-rigid holders
Team bags
Bubble mailers
Extra protection for higher-value cards
Lost or Delayed Parcels

If your parcel is delayed or appears lost, please contact us and we will do our best to assist.

Once an order has been handed to the shipping provider, delivery timeframes are outside of our direct control.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is correct before placing an order.

HoloVault is not responsible for orders sent to incorrectly entered addresses provided during checkout.

Questions

If you have any questions about shipping, packaging, or delivery options, please contact us through the HoloVault contact page.
